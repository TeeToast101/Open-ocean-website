Depth : 200-1000 M

Information :

The mesopelagic zone, the middle pelagic or twilight zone is the part of the pelagic zone that lies between the epipelagic and the bathypelagic zones.It is defined by light, and begins at the depth where only 1% of incident light reaches and ends where there is no light. The temperature variations are large; from over 20°C at the upper layers to around 4°C at the lower layers.
