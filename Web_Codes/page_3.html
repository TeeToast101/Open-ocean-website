Depth : 1000-4000 M

Information :

The bathypelagic zone or bathyal zone is the part of the open ocean.The bathypelagic is also as the midnight zone because of the lack of sunlight; this feature doesn't allow for photosynthesis-driven primary production, preventing growth of phytoplankton or aquatic plants.Human knowledge of this zone remains limited by ability to explore the deep ocean.
