Depth : 6000+ M

Information :

The hadal zone, also known as the hadopelagic zone is the deepest region of the ocean, lying within oceanic trenches.The hadal zone ranges from around 6 to 11 km below sea level and exists in long, narrow, topographic V-shaped depressions.Most hadal habitat is found in the Pacific Ocean.
